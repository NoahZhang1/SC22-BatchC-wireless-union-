  • The Project

    The Lorem Ipsum bot is a text generation bot that acts as a writing assistant for the user. The user will be able to select from a drop-down of different scenarios and the bot will auto-fill certain sentences based on the scenario given.

  • The Dataset

    We split up into three different subgroups to work on training three different models: Human Conversation, Customer Support, and Academics. For the Human Conversation they used various datasets to train their model. To train the model for human conversation they used multiple human conversation datasets from Kaggle. For the Customer Support they used a dataset from Kaggle based on over twenty thousand possible questions and responses that any customer may ask. The Academic Team used a question and answer dataset between a student and a teacher, and a dataset that included over 7,000 research papers on neural networks.

  • The Model

    We are using GPT-2-based AI text generation to autofill sentences. We imported these packages into our code and use them to accomplish our goal. We made use of several datasets that can be found online on websites like Kaggle and Huggingface. These datasets have accurate and diverse data for us to use to train our models. Each team used the pandas library in python to edit their data, and we used bootstrap to help create our website.

  • Use Cases and Expansions

    This text generation project can be used in an assortment of problems, such as conversations. You can use this in any conversation apps, it can generate commonly used text to complete your sentence. It also has a grammar corrector built in.

  • Challenges Faced

    We had some challenges along the way. Training the AI model took a very long time due to webpage issues and large dataset files, this slowed us down a decent amount. We also had some issues with Google Colaboratory, such as us having to reload the files and code blocks every time that we would reopen the project.
